# learnPrompt4

"Create a compact cheat sheet on [topic] that I can read in 5 minutes.
Structure it as follows: (1) a 2 to 3 sentence plain language summary at the top, (2) the 5 to 8 most important concepts as bullet points with a one line explanation each, (3) 2 to 3 concrete real world examples, (4) a simple text based diagram or comparison table if relevant, and (5) the 3 most common mistakes or misconceptions.
Keep everything concise and prioritize clarity over completeness."
