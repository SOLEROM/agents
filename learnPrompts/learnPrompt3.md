# learnPrompt3

"I need to learn [topic] fast. My current level is [beginner/intermediate/advanced]. Build me a 20 hour study plan focused on the 20% of concepts that drive 80% of real world results. Divide it into 10 two hour sessions.
For each session: name the core concept to focus on, list 1 to 2 specific free resources (videos, articles, or documentation), and end with a 15 minute active review (flashcards, summary writing, or practice problem). Prioritize practical application over theory."
