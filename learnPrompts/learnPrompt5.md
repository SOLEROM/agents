# learnPrompt5

"List the top 5 resources for learning [topic], suited for a [beginner/intermediate/advanced] learner.
For each resource, include: (1) the title and type (book, video, course, person/mentor), (2) why it's particularly effective for learning this topic, (3) roughly how long it takes to consume, and (4) whether it's free or paid. Prioritize resources that are widely available, up to date, and known for clarity. Avoid listing resources you're uncertain still exist or are accessible."
