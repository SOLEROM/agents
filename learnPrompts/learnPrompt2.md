# learnPrompt2

"We're going to use the Feynman Technique to help me deeply understand [topic]. Here's how it will work:
Step 1: Explain [topic] to me in the simplest possible terms, using plain everyday language and no jargon, as if I'm hearing about it for the very first time.
Step 2: After your explanation, stop and ask me to re explain the concept back to you in my own words. Wait for my response before continuing.
Step 3: Identify any gaps, misunderstandings, or missing pieces in my explanation. Re teach only those specific gaps.
Step 4: Repeat steps 2 to 3 until I can explain the full concept accurately and clearly without prompting. Begin with Step 1 now."
